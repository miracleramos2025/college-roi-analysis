# Figures Directory

This folder contains all the visualizations and figures generated for the final report. Each file represents a specific analysis or exploration performed during the project.

## Contents
1. **`avg_mid_career_salary_by_region.png`**
   - Visualization of average mid-career salaries across different regions.

2. **`avg_salary_growth_by_undergrad.png`**
   - Comparison of salary growth across different undergraduate majors.

3. **`avg_starting_salary_table.png`**
   - Graphical representation of the table summarizing starting salaries by institution type.

4. **`earnings_growth_by_institution_type.png`**
   - Line plot showing earnings growth over time based on institution type.

5. **`earnings_vs_debt_scatterplot.png`**
   - Scatter plot comparing median debt and earnings by institution type.

6. **`med_salary_by_gender_field_of_study.png`**
   - Bar chart comparing median salaries by gender for selected fields of study.

7. **`median_earnings_by_credential.png`**
   - Bar chart illustrating median earnings for different credential levels.

8. **`median_salary_by_field_of_study.png`**
   - Bar chart showcasing median salaries for various fields of study three years post-graduation.

9. **`pell_grants_vs_earnings_boxplot.png`**
   - Box plot comparing the impact of Pell Grant status on earnings three years after graduation.

10. **`random_scatter_pell_grants_plot.png`**
    - Scatter plot showing the effect of Pell Grants on median earnings for a random selection of fields of study.
